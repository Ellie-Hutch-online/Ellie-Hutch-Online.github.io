Guitar Lessons

Lesson 1: Basic Open Chords

Learn the most common open chords: C, G, D, E, and A. These are the building blocks for many songs.

Lesson 2: Strumming Patterns

Practice different strumming patterns like down-down-up-up-down to add rhythm to your playing.

Lesson 3: Reading Tabs

Understand how to read guitar tablature to play your favorite songs easily.

Lesson 4: Basic Fingerpicking

Start with simple fingerpicking patterns to create beautiful melodies.
