Beginner Guitar Chords

C Major

One of the first chords to learn, the C major chord is bright and happy. Use your ring finger on the 3rd fret of the 5th string.

G Major

A popular chord used in many songs. Place your middle finger on the 3rd fret of the 6th string.

D Major

This chord sounds bright and clear. Use your index finger on the 2nd fret of the 3rd string.

E Minor

A simple chord to start with. Place your middle and ring fingers on the 2nd fret of the 5th and 4th strings.

A Minor

A soft sounding chord. Use your index, middle, and ring fingers on the 1st, 2nd, and 2nd frets of the 2nd, 3rd, and 4th strings.
